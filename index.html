About Peru

Peru is a country on the west coast of South America with an amazing mix of landscapes: coast, mountains, and jungle. It has a long history shaped by ancient civilizations like the Incas. Peru is known for Machu Picchu, Lake Titicaca, and beautiful cities such as Lima, Cusco, and Arequipa. Peruvian food is famous around the world, and the people are known for their warmth and strong community values.
