About Peru

Peru is a country located on the west coast of South America with a rich cultural heritage. It is known for its diverse geography, including the coast, mountains, and jungle. Peru has a long history influenced by ancient civilizations such as the Incas. The capital city is Lima, while Cusco is one of the most visited tourist destinations. Peruvian cuisine is recognized worldwide for its variety and unique flavors. The country is home to famous landmarks like Machu Picchu and Lake Titicaca. Peru has many traditional dances, music styles, and colorful clothing. Its people are known for their warmth, resilience, and strong sense of community.
